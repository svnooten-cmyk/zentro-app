# Zentro Design Language

Version: 1.0

---

# Philosophy

Zentro is not a social media platform.

Zentro is an ecosystem.

Every design decision should strengthen that ecosystem.

---

# Core Principles

## 1. The world comes first.

The map is the heart of every World.

Users should always feel connected to the place they are exploring.

---

## 2. Everything starts with a pin.

Pins are the visual language of Zentro.

Every contribution starts at a place.

---

## 3. Less interface. More world.

The interface should never dominate the experience.

Glass components keep the world visible.

---

## 4. One action. One purpose.

Every button has a single clear purpose.

No duplicate actions.

No unnecessary complexity.

---

## 5. Contributions strengthen the ecosystem.

Every Zentro should add value.

The goal is helping, inspiring or connecting others.

---

## 6. Recognition over explanation.

Users should recognize actions through consistent icons and interaction patterns.

Avoid unnecessary text whenever possible.

---

## 7. Consistency beats creativity.

Once a component exists, reuse it.

Never redesign an existing interaction without a clear reason.

---

## 8. Calm by default.

The interface should feel spacious.

Breathing room is part of the experience.

---

## 9. The user is exploring.

Users are not scrolling endlessly.

Users are exploring the world.

---

## 10. Every World follows the same language.

Travel is the first World.

Future Worlds follow exactly the same design principles.
